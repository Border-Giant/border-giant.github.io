We're Locals Helping Locals

We started Border Giant to save local businesses time and money when importing and exporting goods for their customers.

They face substantial challenges not only due to the high costs and slow shipping times that Canadian Post requires but also because of knowledge barriers with filling out all the required legal shipment documentation.

Clearly, a better way is needed, and by registering as an international commercial carrier, a customs broker, and freight forwarding agency (just to name a few), Border Giant is able to bring parcels over the US border and ship them directly from the USA avoiding the use of Canada post altogether.

As a result, goods are shipped faster, better, and cheaper. All while saving you the headache of filling out complex legal forms by using our automated shipping software solution.
